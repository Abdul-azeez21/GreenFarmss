About us

About the idea

Toriola farms prides itself in providing a range of products, grown or manufatured under conditions supervised by professionals. With emphasis on safety and quality, we adopt accessible sustainable systems and carry out continuous research and development to ensure constant improvements across the food value chain.

Our Mission

To foster food security by aligning and optimizing the food value chain processe to provide quality products at reasonable prices.

Our Vision

To be a leader in the African agribusiness space, while creating employment by engaging local labour force from cultivation to processing of agricultural products.
